AI Taxonomy

The AI taxonomy is a structured classification system that organizes the various capabilities and components of Artificial Intelligence (AI) into a coherent framework.

It includes the motivation behind the AI, guides and directives and possible risks and gaps we should be aware of.

The AI Taxonomy makes it easier to navigate the vast and dynamic field of AI.
